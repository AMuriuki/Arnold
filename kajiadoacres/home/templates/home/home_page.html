Your Property Experts

kajiado acres is a collaboration between lawyers, surveyors, and land sellers in and around Kajiado county, in an effort to promote transparency, honesty, and legitimacy in land dealings. Land sellers especially those from the Maasai community tend to be illiterate and fall victim to unscrupulous real estate agents. Kajiado acres facilitates direct dealing between the seller and the buyer, as well as offers initial free consultations to potential land buyers.
